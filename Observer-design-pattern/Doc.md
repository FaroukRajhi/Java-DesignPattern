When we need many other objects to receive an update when another objects changes.

- Stock market with thousands of stocks needs to send updates to objects representing individual stocks.

- publisher-subscriber architecture.

- publisher: Is the subject that sends many stocks to the observers.
- subscribers: Is the observer that takes the ones they want and use them.

*****

So The observer pattern is a software design pattern in which an object, called the subject, maintains a list of its dependents, called observers, and notifies them automatically of any state changes, usually by calling one of their methods.
